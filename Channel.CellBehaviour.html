      Channel.CellBehaviour behaviour (elixtor v0.1.0-beta)

      Defines behaviour for converting between binary cells and specific cell types. Cells are differentiated by their command code.

      Tor provides a list of cell types with hyperlinks to the relevant sections of the Tor specification in section 2.3.2 "Cells (messages and channels)".
